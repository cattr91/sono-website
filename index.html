Capabilities

Where we listen

We focus on the utility systems that drive energy waste in data centers: cooling, power and on-site energy assets. We work with your existing operational data and historian signals.

Chilled Water Optimisation

Setpoints fixed at commissioning and never revisited. Sono detects headroom and recommends safe upward adjustments, reducing compressor load without thermal risk.

Primary pilot entry point

CRAH & Airflow

Detect recirculation hot spots, over-cooling conditions and fan inefficiencies across computer room air handlers. Ranked recommendations on supply temperature and airflow balance.

Cooling stack

Power Draw & PUE

Continuous monitoring of IT load versus facility overhead. Identifies UPS inefficiency windows, PDU load imbalance and operational levers to improve PUE.

Power

Energy Orchestration

Data centers increasingly operate behind-the-meter assets: BESS, generators, solar and wind. Sono reads signals across these and grid consumption to surface dispatch recommendations, turning sunk capex into active flexibility.

How it works

Three phases of autonomy

We earn the right to act. Sono starts fully supervised and extends autonomy only as it proves value and builds operator trust.

01

Read

We connect to your existing BMS and historian infrastructure. No new hardware. No instrumentation project. Chilled water temps, CRAH setpoints, power draw: ingested from day one.

Phase 1 · Supervised
02

Understand

Sono continuously monitors facility conditions, detects anomalies before alarms fire, and explains what is happening and why. Every signal connected. Nothing left unread.

Phase 1 · Supervised
03

Act

Ranked recommendations reach operators with supporting evidence. Every decision (accepted, deferred, or dismissed) becomes training data. By month three, Sono knows your facility's risk tolerance, not just its physics.

The product

From anomaly to action in fourteen minutes.

Sono reads your existing historian, detects anomalies before alarms fire, traces root cause to specific equipment, and surfaces a plain-language recommendation with a confidence score. Fully supervised in Phase 1 — one click closes the loop.

No new hardware. No integration project. Historian-native. Physics-constrained so no physically impossible setpoint can ever be suggested.
